2015 Speakers

Shri Abhayanand

He is an IPS officer and flourishingly served as Director General of Police in Bihar government. He is an educationalist who single handedly conceptualized Super30 classroom program to teach underprivileged to crack IITJEE.
Through is motivational talk, he brought revolutionary ideas in the minds of the audience persuading them to selflessly serve the nation. He raised the morale of all the earnest listeners to become responsible citizens and interlinked the relationship between Society, State and Spiritualism.


Leena Bansal

She is popularly known as Miss Walking Shoes, is a globe trotter, a crusader and an adrenaline junkie who quit her job and went on a fanatical self funded solo expedition to 32 countries across the globe.
The audience was truly enamoured of her vivacious attitude as she brought a zealous, lively and exuberant spirited environment inside the venue. The listeners were exhilarated as she inspired them to explore the world beyond horizons.


Guruji Syed Sallauddin Pasha

He is a two time National Award recipient, a Guinness Record holder and an internationally recognized cerebral brand for avant-grade ‘therapeutic theatre’ productions for persons with special needs.
He acquainted the attendees with fine works in his foundation, ‘Ability Unlimited’ which trained disabled students therapeutic dance theatre on wheelchair. He sensitized the young citizens and inspired them to practice such innovative ideas for the social welfare.


Neeti Palta

She is s a famous female stand up comedian who was ranked first among the top 20 stand up comedians of India in 2014. She was the first Indian to perform at Melbourne Comedy Festival in 2013.
She filled the room with her jaunty, light hearted, unparalleled sense of humour as her hysterical gigs possessed the power of cheering up the youngsters. She also motivated the students to follow their dream and work towards it perseveringly.


Ankon Mitra

He is also known as the torch bearer for Origami in India, resurged the ancient technique and spreading it through the innumerable domains. He is a Gold Medallist from School of Planning and Architecture and a renowned Origami artist.
He captivated the audience with his works and performed some of the Japanese art traditions which held them spell bound. He believes that just like origami is about creating on exquisite object by folding and unfolding a square sheet, our universe too constantly folds and unfolds into innumerable things.


Seema Rao

She is an iconic youthful citizen who became India’s first woman commando trainer, having trained India Special Forces for 18 years without compensation. She is a certified doctor in conventional medicine and also has a MBA in crises management. She is an HMI medallist and a 7th degree Black Belt in MMA.
She drove the audience by her altering determination inculcating in them a sense of national service to bring pride to the country. She persuaded the audience on changing their perception towards women by speaking on the topic ‘Women Bash on Regardless’ and held the attendees spell bound after enlightening them with her contribution towards women empowerment.


Tirath Das Dogra

He is an eminent Indian forensic pathologist, a former director of AIIMS and an authority on forensic medicine. His work has been instrumental in many outlandish cases which required his forensic investigations.
He acquainted the attendees with his noticeable works in the field of medical sciences. His voice took the TEDxMSIT stage to a whole new pinnacle as he spoke about Justice by present Forensic approaches in India by showcasing Kashmir and Nithari serial killings.


Srijan Mahajan

He is a celebrated musician who has been associated with the popular rock and roll band Parikrama since 2007. He has played on a number of esteemed stages in his career and has also been associated notorious Delhi bands.
The attendees admired his fanatic expedition towards achieving his goal to master his drumming skills and to create a storm in the Rock music circuit. His band Parikrama has played over 3000 concerts and has performed on esteemed worldwide stages.


Chad R Norberg

He is the Trade and Technology Unit Head at the Embassy of United States Of America in India. He is an ardent visionary with a motive of strengthening the Indo-US tries.
His sincere fidelity towards the bond of India and the United States has flowered in boundless congeniality between the two nations. His efforts on bridging the gap between developing and developed countries are commendable. Through his talk he envisioned India in 2050 and he scrutinized the nation as a world leader; politically, military, economically, and culturally. Mr. Norberg sees India as a confederation whose economy has the potential to eliminate poverty not only in its own country, but across Asia. 


Gurlad Singh

He is a respected chairman estate of Delhi Sikh Gurudwara Prabandhak Committee. He is an embodiment of calmness, generosity and is enriched with high moral values.
He held the audience spellbound by acknowledging them with constant efforts for the welfare of the people. He mobilized the attendees to uphold the spirit of service before self. The audience raised their hat to admire is noticeable works towards community service.
